Why is learning continuous?

Never stop

If you want to succeed in your studies, it's crucial that you adopt a "never stop" mindset. Studying consistently and continuously is the key to progress and achieving your goals.

Just return

If you've stopped or lost momentum in any activity or project, know that it's never too late to start again. Sometimes, circumstances can change, or we may feel demotivated or overwhelmed.

Follow your own pace

Advancing little by little maintains steady progress towards goals, avoiding stopping or giving up. Even small steps bring us closer to the destination and motivate continuity. Do not underestimate the power of each action toward the final goal.
